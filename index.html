AI Product · Undergraduate Thesis · Live

Cognitive Study Assistant

View live ↗

Problem

Students default to one study method regardless of their mental state, so focus and retention suffer — a behavior-change problem, not an information problem.

Solution

A web app that selects the right study protocol (Deep Work vs. Pomodoro) from the user's sleep data, primes focus with AI-generated warm-up questions per subject, and scores retention through active-recall analysis after each session.

Impact

Live in production on zero-cost infrastructure. Privacy-first by design: no accounts, no stored personal data — which removed the biggest adoption barrier.

Lessons

Owning a product end to end — from behavioral research and scoping through LLM integration to deployment — is the fastest way to learn where AI genuinely adds value.
